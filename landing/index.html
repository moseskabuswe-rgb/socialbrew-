How it works

Built for two kinds of
coffee people.

Coffee Lovers

Discover indie shops near you.

01Find independent coffee shops near you, sorted by proximity. Zero chains ever included.
02Rate your visit using our signature Mug Fill Score — drag to fill the mug based on how good it really was.
03Share brews, follow other coffee lovers, and build a personal map of every shop you've visited.
Shop Owners

Get discovered by people who actually care.

01Your shop appears in our discovery feed when customers are nearby — no paid placement, ever.
02Every brew your customers post builds your reputation organically — real ratings from real visits.
03Appear in City Pulse — our weekly trending shops list that puts the best indie spots front and center.
For Shop Owners

Your shop is already
here. Claim it.

Social Brew is free for independent coffee shops. Claim your listing and get access to your owner portal — post updates, set up a punch card loyalty program, and connect with the customers who already love your shop.

01
Find your shop on Social Brew and tap "Claim it." Takes 60 seconds.
02
We verify your ownership and send you a portal invite within 2 business days.
03
Post updates, run punch cards, and build loyalty with your regulars — all free.
